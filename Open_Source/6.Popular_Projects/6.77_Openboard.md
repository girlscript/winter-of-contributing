OpenBoard is an interactive whiteboard software which is compatible with any projector and pointing device.It was primarily designed for use in schools and universities. It is a free and open-source software which was originally forked from Open-Sankore. The initial version was released in 2013.

## Where is it from?

OpenBoard was originally forked from Open-Sankor√© 2.0, which was itself based on Uniboard. This fork was created to refocus the software on its original core functionalities and values, that is the work of a teacher in a classroom, privileging the ease of use. The evolution of this software will therefore take place in accordance with these core principles.
